May '23

Pre-Announce (May 29th)

Press Release on Twitter, BitcoinTalk and launch of the public JMES Discord, enabling validators who want to join to prepare.

Validator Launch (June 5 th)

Open and fair launch of the validator set, genesis block, announcement on Discord, Twitter and Bitcointalk.

Initial DAO Proposals (May 29 - June 12th)

Launching the Governance App, the UI to enable creation of funding proposals for DAOs and voting by JMES holders

June '23

Launch Party, Berlin (12th June)

Web3 party with the crypto crowd in Berlin, onboarding people to JMES. Join us to meet the JMES team and mingle with the movers and shakers in the Berlin Web3 scene.

DAO Voting (June 12th-June 26th)

Voting starts on whichever DAOs were proposed in the first two weeks. All voting happens directly on the blockchain.

DAO Funding Starts (26th June)

After 2 weeks of voting, the results are in! The protocol tallies the results and begins to fund the winning DAOs with their share of the block reward, effectively mining the chain. Go decentralized funding!

Q3-Q4 '23

JMES Mobile Wallet Release

Get set for the Official JMES App on iOS and Android. Enables anyone to signup to JMES with a unique username, store and spend their JMES online and at our venues securely. Stay tuned!

Tokenizing Off-Chain Art

Tokeziing High-Value Artworks from off-chain collectors

Onboarding On-Chain Artists

Onboarding up and coming Artists onto our chain via governance incentives and tokenizing their Art

Bootstrapping Community DAOs

Helping community DAOs to get kickstarted using our decentralized blockchain governance and funding system

Q1-Q4 '24

Launch first JMES Marketplace (Q1)

Authentic ownership and trading of fractions of the real-world assets tokenized in 2023 and beyond, via the first Art Dealer DAO launch, accessed directly from the JMES Mobile App.

First Community Museum

Launching the first Museum to showcase community owned assets on JMES in Berlin.

First Artist Incubator

Launching the first residence for our on-chain Artists to work together, connect directly to the JMES Community and earn a living directly from the blockchain.
